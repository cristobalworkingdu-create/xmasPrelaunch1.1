Holiday Bring the Workout Home · Origin Story

When winter bites outside, move the training ground into a warm living room

For those who stay committed, winter’s low temperatures are the toughest rival. Biting wind and icy air reduce performance and turn every trip outdoors into a test of willpower. To avoid losing momentum in the cold, we need a warmer, more focused training plan.

MERACH helps you relocate “winter training” indoors. Skip the fight against the chill and continue your program with the R28 rower and stepper in the comfort of your living room. Keep the sweat and heart rate steady so you stay in peak form and fully charged for the holiday season.
